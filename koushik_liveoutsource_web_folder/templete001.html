Meaning "support" in Sanskrit. Adhara is a women's club and space that offers professional, social, spiritual and charitable programs that celebrate women and enhance their development.

Adhara is a place where women are free to mingle, socialize, network, pamper themselves, break a sweat, meditate, stretch, relax, work, learn, read, invent and give back.

An environment for women to make powerful social and professional connections, unlock their potential, nurture their health and well being, and use their unique talents to give back and serve others.

INTENTION

Adhara was built for women to congregate, collaborate and create. Within our walls, women help each other lead satisfying lives and talk about things they are passionate about, share ideas, and inspire each other to be bold and take action.

We are a social group by nature but we are also socially aware. We invest in our own Adhara women right here in New York City as well as women around the globe because we know that when women are economic agents and leaders, social change accelerates, returns multiply, and can be measured in our families, our neighborhoods, and on the other side of the world.

TEAM

Alaina Gurwitz
Founder

Alaina has an ever-growing passion for creating programs that impact women and children around the globe. She is inspired and motivated by women who are taking charge of their lives in living within their passions and dreams. Before dreaming up Adhara and living within her own dreams, she worked on international development projects throughout Africa, the Middle East and the Caribbean on health, education, and gender equality programs at Columbia University. Alaina holds a Master of Social Work from Columbia University and a Bachelor of Fine Arts from New York University. PROGRAMS

Adhara marks the beginning of a change in how women network, socialize and grow. As a womenís club and space, we are dedicated to offering female-focused events and programs that appeal to women on many levels. We are a community of women ages 21 and older, with a particular focus on professional women ages 25 - 65.

Through our programs, Adhara creates and empowers a community of women while strengthening opportunities for personal, professional, spiritual, and charitable growth. We help women form networks and encourage them to seek out role models that can help catapult them into the next phase of their lives, filled with authenticity, contentment and success.

SPACE

Adhara's home, opening in late 2013, will be a space that evokes openness, peace, creativity, and vibrancy. Adhara is unique in that it will integrate the best features of a members-only club, spa, office, gym, and yoga studio into a sophisticated and welcoming environment for women.

The future space will include:

  • A friendly and inviting reception and waiting area
  • Numerous spacious and lively lounges and multi-purpose event spaces for socializing and attending professional and social events
  • A quiet, yet creativity-filled work room
  • A plush media room and library
  • Multiple open and airy yoga and dance studios
  • A private and peaceful meditation room
  • A health conscious cafe and bar
  • A private ladies' changing and locker room

EVENTS

Adhara women are energetic, passionate and committed, and have a deep interest in developing themselves socially and spiritually. We come from all walks of life, all industries and are all ages.
